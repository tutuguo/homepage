The South is Not an Island

Visual Design for the exhibition the South Is Not an Island in OCAT Shenzhen

Tutu designed a visual for the exhibition the South Is Not an Island in OCAT Shenzhen. The exhibition target to have an academic discussion about the art atmosphere in Canton---- south China. If not an island, what is the South? As someone grew up there, Tutu sees south China as archipelagos surrounded by a rich water system culturally, politically, and geographically. On the design, the big titles flow as if they are floating in the water which builds connections among the archipelagos.
