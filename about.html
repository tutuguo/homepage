About Me

I like to use design to create interaction and communication with people,
to encourage people to look at issues from a totally different perspective,
to trigger people to stop, to take a break, to watch, and to think.
I like to make things which can move, like short movies, motions,
or which can trigger people to move, like interaction devices. I am a designer/writer/curator/analogue photographer.
I am currently living in Rotterdam, the Netherlands.
I grew up in Guangzhou, a huge city in south China.
And I moved here for my bachelor's study when I was 18.
